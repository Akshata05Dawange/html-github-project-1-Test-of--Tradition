🥮 Puranpoli

Puranpoli is a sweet flatbread, traditionally prepared during Maharastrian festivals like Holi,Gudipadva,Makarsankranti and Ganesh Chaturthi.....

Ingredients:

  • 🥣Chana dal (split chickpeas) – 1 cup
  • 🍯Jaggery – 1 cup
  • 🌿 Cardamom powder – ½ tsp
  • 🌾Wheat flour – 1.5 cups
  • 🧈Ghee – 2 tbsp

🌶️ Vada Pav

Often called the “Indian Burger”, Vada Pav is a popular street food from Maharashtra, especially Mumbai.

Ingredients:

  • Spicy mashed potato fritter (vada)
  • Pav (bread bun)
  • Chutneys
  • Fried green chilies
